The story of my life

My name is Luciano, and for that many may think I'm italian. Well, I was born in Brazil and I lived most of my life in a city located right in the center of the South America. When growing up, I was mostly interested in making friends, watching cartoons and solving logic puzzles.

By the time I turned 10, my family bought our first computer. I became instantly obsessed with that. So that, a year later I already knew how to format it and replace hardware and I/O parts. I would spare any time I have left learning more about computers and browsing the internet in search of more knowledge.

When I was 16, I started working with computers' troubleshooting and repair and I still do it to this day. My only clients at the time were friends and family and I was doing it as a hobby. As it grew, I would only improve my skillset.

When I finished school, I decided I wanted to make our planet a better place or, at least, help it keep favorable to human existence. That's why I graduated in Environmental Engineering and worked for almost 6 years in this field.

Even after all this time working in the field, my passion for Information Technology never faded away. That's why when my wife and I received the invitation to move to Canada, the first thing that crossed my mind was going back to my roots and work with technology.
